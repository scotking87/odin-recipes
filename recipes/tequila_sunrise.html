Tequila Sunrise

Description

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz tequila
  • 8 oz Orange Juice
  • 0.75 oz Grenadine
Optional
  • Maraschino cherries
  • Orange slices

Steps

  1. Partially fill glass with ice
  2. Pour half of orange juice, the tequila, then remaining orange juice
  3. Place lip of spoon on edge of glass
  4. Pour the greneadine onto the spoon, allowing it to slide down the glass onto the ice
  5. After grenadine has settled, add additional garnish
