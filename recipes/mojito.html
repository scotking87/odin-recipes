Mojito

Description

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz rum
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 0.75 oz lime juice
  • 4-8 oz club soda
  • 6 fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. Add mint and simple syrup to mixer, and briefly muddle
  2. Add the rest of the ingrediates, and shake
  3. Strain drink onto ice
  4. Top with club soda to desired strength and taste
  5. Garnish with mint and lime
